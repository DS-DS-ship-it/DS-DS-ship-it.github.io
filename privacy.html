Privacy Policy

This site hosts browser-based creative tools (“apps”) that run on your device. We do not run a server that collects personal data from you.

Information we collect

  • No account data: We do not require sign-in and we do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • No server logging: This site is served via GitHub Pages. We do not operate custom server-side logging.

Local device data

Some app settings and optional saved state may be stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data stays on your device unless you clear your browser storage.

Microphone / media permissions

If you enable microphone access, audio is processed in your browser to drive visual effects. We do not transmit microphone audio to a server. You can revoke permission anytime in your browser settings.

We may display ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads, and to personalize ads where permitted by law.
